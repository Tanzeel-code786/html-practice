About Us

Modern Public School was established in 1998 to provide quality education to all. Located in a peaceful area, it offers a perfect learning environment. The school started with just 5 classrooms and now has over 40. It is known for its excellent academic results and discipline. Modern facilities include computer labs, science labs, and a digital library. Students actively participate in sports, debates, and cultural programs. The school promotes modern teaching methods with smart classes. Its mission is to build confident, skilled, and responsible citizens.

Courses We Offer

  • Nursery to Grade 12
  • Science, Arts & Commerce Streams
  • Computer & IT Basics
  • Spoken English & Personality Development
  • Sports & Extracurricular Activities
