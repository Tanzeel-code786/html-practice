Course: SEO

Description

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving a website's visibility on search engines. It helps websites rank higher in search results, driving more organic (free) traffic. SEO includes keyword research, content optimization, link building, and technical improvements. Both on-page and off-page strategies are used to enhance search engine rankings. It's a powerful digital marketing skill with strong demand in online business and freelancing.

  • Off Page SEO
